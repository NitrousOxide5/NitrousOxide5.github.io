The Best Oral Hygiene Products For a Clean and Healthy Mouth

By Ahmed  |  May 11, 2021  |  Advice

That is the time when people run toward buying the best dental products. Hence it is always better to take care of oral health as early as possible. It is not just health but also the confidence that a good set of white teeth provide. A radiant smile is one of the biggest factors that add to the attractiveness of a person.

Today we have a range of the best oral care products that are affordable as well as effective. Here in this blog, we lay out the important practices for maintaining oral health while presenting the 10 best oral hygiene products that you must look for when you need to brighten your smile further.

Important Practices For Oral Health

Dental care products are important but maintaining the right oral health practices is even more important. Even if you buy the most technologically advanced toothbrush, what good will it do if you don’t let it touch your teeth? With an online medical store like AMS, buying oral care products is easy. The only thing you have to take care of is your oral hygiene discipline. Here are some useful tips for that

  • BBB - Brush Before Bed

Yes, everybody knows this but still finding 10 minutes for brushing teeth seems hard when you have the option to do something important like scrolling Instagram. The food stuck between teeth which remains overnight causes bacteria buildup resulting in teeth problems like cavities and bad breath. Having a brushing habit before going to bed can help you keep your mouth healthy and clean with minimum efforts.

  • Keep Calm and Drink Water

70% of the body is made up of water yet some people try to survive at a lower percentage. It’s just not about a healthy mouth but also the overall health of the body. A dry mouth facilitates the buildup of plaque on the teeth as well as the cracks on the tongue. Chronic dryness of the mouth can lead to the weakening of gums and teeth due to bacteria buildup. That is why it becomes important to drink ample water, also in mouth moisturiser can also be used to counter this.

  • No to Too Much Sweetness

The craving for sweet food is often termed as having a sweet tooth, however, sweets can become the cause of cavities. Especially when the bits of sweets are left inside the mouth without brushing, it becomes a whole party for bacterias inside the mouth. For people who are already having dental problems, it is good to avoid or minimize sweets until things get better.

  • Clean the Tongue

Every piece of food goes inside the human body with the tongue tasting it first. However, generally brushing is considered the only measure for oral care for a clean and healthy mouth. But scraping the tongue is also an important practice to maintain and enhance the tasting abilities of the tongue. While gently scraping the tongue also helps in removing the white buildup on the tongue helping to retain back the original color.

  • Visit a Dentist 

When a person is already suffering from dental care problems, it is best to consult a qualified dentist to buy oral care products.  There is no better alternative to expert medical advice when it comes to dealing with health issues. Further, with online medical stores like AMS, you can easily get an online prescription without any hassle.

Now that we know about important oral care practices, we can look at some of the best oral health care products available out there.

Top Oral Hygiene Products

There are all kinds of oral care available out there but it is important to know about products that can be best for daily care as well as for an emergency. Here we present out an oral care products list that can come in handy if you are looking for oral hygiene products.

  1. David Craig Thymol Mouthwash

When it comes to taking care of bad breath and maintaining freshness all day long, David Craig Thymol Mouthwash is one of the best dental hygiene products. The product uses a thymol compound which helps to fight the bacterias in the mouth. To use it, mix it with 7 parts of water and gargle with it. However, make sure it is not to be swallowed.

  1. Oral 7 Moisturising Mouth Gel

Offered by the Oral 7 brand, the moisturising mouth gel is the one-stop solution for all mouth dryness problems. The gel uses an original and clinically proven formula that incorporates enzymes into the product. These enzymes are similar to the ones that are found in healthy saliva. This is to be used only in the night time while applying it with the tongue to the mouth.

  1. Betadine Sore Throat Gargle

Gargling normally with warm water is referred to as an essential part of a best oral care routine. While a lot of time people face sore throat problems due to cold and other reasons. In this condition, it is good to use a solely focused product for treating sore throat. Betadine sore throat gargle is to be used by diluting it with 1ml to 20 ml of water and gargling for 30 seconds.

 

  1. Zovirax Duo Cold Sore Cream

Cold sores become a big problem while eating and drinking. Further, some people are more prone to cold sores. Zovirax Duo Cold Sore Cream is a great solution to tackle this. With its acyclovir and hydrocortisone formula, it works wonders on inflammation and helps in faster treatment. The product should only be used by people who are 12 years of age or older.

  1. Colgate Neutra Flour 5000 For Sensitive Teeth

Sensitive teeth can put a person in very uncomfortable situations in a social setting. Further, it can also become problematic in the long term. Colgate is a popular branded oral care product that has been serving a wide range of products. The Colgate Neutrafluor 5000 relieves from dental hypersensitivity and root exposure. For best results, it is to be used with a soft bristle toothbrush.

 

  1. Sensodyne Daily Care Sensitive Toothpaste

Sensodyne specializes in products that are focused on treating the problem of dental hypersensitivity. The Sensodyne Daily Care Sensitive Toothpaste is one of the top oral care products when it comes to sensitivity. It contains potassium nitrate, hydrated silica, glycerine and other key ingredients which help in soothing the nerves connected to the teeth. The paste should be used regularly with a soft bristle toothbrush for best results.

  1. Goldx Oil of Cloves

Sudden toothaches can be very painful and if not taken care they may get even worse. But a simple remedy like clove oil has been found to give great results in this case. Goldx oil of cloves is extracted from high-quality cloves which help in relief from toothache and cavities. To use it, dip a small cotton bud in clove oil and apply it to the affected tooth.

  1. Cepacaine Oral Solution

Sore throat and mouth infections can be painful at times, in such a case gargling with Cepacaine Oral Solution can be a great remedy. Cepacaine oral solution has been a popular product due to its anesthetic effect. It also used to provide relief after dental products and surgeries. The product should only be used by a person who is 6 years of age or older.

  1. Steradent Denture Cream Ultra

Maintaining denture is a crucial part of oral hygiene. The Steradent Denture Cream Ultra is specially offered to help people with dentures. The Steradent Denture Cream Ultra secures the denture while stopping the food particles from getting stuck in the dentures. The dental cream needs to be applied in small dots while nothing should be eaten or drunk up to at least 10 minutes after the application.

  1. Nyal Toothache Drops

Containing active ingredients Ethanol, Phenol and Benzocaine, the Nyal Toothache Drops is one of the top oral hygiene products when dealing with toothache. The toothache drops need to be applied with the help of cotton buds. It is also important to avoid sensitive areas like the eyes. The product needs to be stored below 25 degrees for best effectiveness.
