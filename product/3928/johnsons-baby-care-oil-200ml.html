General Information

Johnson's Baby Oil 200ml is enriched with vitamin E. Johnson's Baby Oil 200ml helps keep the baby's skin healthy and soft through the entire duration. It is 100% clinically proven and is mild and gentle for babies. It is dermatologist and allergy tested. It has been passed through 5 levels of safety tests. It is prone to dryness and suitable for newborn babies.

USAGE INSTRUCTIONS-

● Use this daily.
● Apply gently on the whole skin before and after bath.
● Pour over full skin and massage mildly.

PRECAUTIONS-

● Avoid contact with eyes.
● If discomfort occurs, then stop the usage and refer to the physicists.
